About Us

With over 60 years of combined experience, Global Urban Strategies, Inc. (Global) has been helping cities, agencies, businesses, and non-pro­fits tell their story to obtain federal and state funding and simplify their external communications. The core of our business consists of Grant Writing and Communications. Global is certifi­ed as a Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise and Small Business Enterprise to help you meet any contracting diversity goals. We have obtained over $500 million in grants and facilitated over 300 stakeholder meetings.

Our services allow our clients the freedom to focus on their projects. At Global, we provide solutions and remove the barriers to your success.

Our experiences

Omar Hernandez President

A bilingual professional with twenty-three years of experience leading companies specialized in governmental advocacy, project management, business administration, strategic planning, marketing, community engagement, and education.

Steven Ly Partner

With over 15 years of corporate experience, Steven Ly has coordinated and facilitated over 100 workshops, community events, and town halls. Bilingual in Chinese, he is also able to interact and communicate with the fastest growing non-English speaking demographic in California.
